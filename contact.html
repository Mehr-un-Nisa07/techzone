Frequently Asked Questions

Are all products on TechZone 100% genuine? ▼
Yes, absolutely. Every product we sell is sourced directly from authorised distributors and comes with an official warranty. We have a strict zero-tolerance policy for counterfeit goods.
How long does delivery take? ▼
Orders are typically delivered within 1–3 business days across major cities in Pakistan including Lahore, Karachi, Islamabad, Faisalabad and Rawalpindi. Remote areas may take 3–5 business days.
What is your return policy? ▼
We offer a 7-day hassle-free return policy on most products. Items must be in original packaging and unused condition. Simply contact us within 7 days of receiving your order and we'll arrange a pickup.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept Cash on Delivery (COD), JazzCash, EasyPaisa, bank transfer, and all major debit/credit cards (Visa, Mastercard). All online payments are 100% secure and encrypted.
Do you offer warranty on products? ▼
Yes! All products come with the manufacturer's official warranty. Smartphones and laptops typically carry 1-year warranties. Warranty details are mentioned on each product page.
Can I track my order? ▼
Yes. Once your order is dispatched you will receive an SMS with a tracking number. You can also contact our support team anytime for a real-time update on your delivery.
