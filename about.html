Our Story

From a Small Shop to Pakistan's #1 Tech Store

TechZone was founded in 2018 in Lahore with one simple goal — to give Pakistani consumers access to genuine, premium electronics at fair prices. What started as a small shop in Gulberg has grown into one of Pakistan's most trusted online electronics destinations.

We got tired of seeing customers get burned by fake products and overpriced retailers. So we built something different — a store where every product is 100% authentic, every price is transparent, and every customer is treated with respect.

Today we serve over 10,000+ customers across Pakistan, ship to every major city, and carry 500+ products across 8 categories. But our values remain exactly the same as day one.
